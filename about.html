About

Cool Cats & awesome anime is an image blog which posts and shares anime and cat pictures/art from official posters, home and amateur photographers/artists. We also host our own encodes of anime series and movies, which are available to download via the torrent links. If you have any copyright concerns, please contact us using the form on the contact page and we will get back to you as soon as possible.


This site was created and developed by a former encoder and QA Manager of the beloved anime streaming site - AnimeFTW. Ever since its unfortunate demise, I have wanted to continue the legacy left behind by AnimeFTW and so created this website and have continued to encode and share anime with the world.


Long live AnimeFTW!
